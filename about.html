About the Game

What does "Taytayan" mean?

Taytayan is came from the word Taytay. It is a Bicolano term (one of the Dialects in the Philippines) for Pantay which means Aligned or Straight. Taytayan now means to align a group of objects in any direction without any order.

What is Taytayan Game?

Taytayan Game is a board game where there are always 2 players playing on each side of the board. The board consists of 3 by 3 squares. The 3 squares on top and the 3 squares on the bottom is the starting point of the pieces while the 3 in the middle is the room for the moves. There are 6 pieces in total and 3 vacant rooms in the middle of the board. The Goal here is to move the pieces and form a straight line such that all 3 pieces are aligned either horizontally, vertically or diagonally. The detailed explanation of the game will be discussed in Game Rules and Mechanics section of the page.

The Origin of the Game

Although the word "Taytayan" is from the Province of Bicol, the Game is actually originated from Metro Manila. it is played most of the Elementary students during 2010 to 2015 and still, it is being played by other children until now but not very often unlike before where children have no access in the internet and digital games.

Game Rules and Mechanics

The Objective of the Game

The main objective here is to position your pieces in a straight path such that all 3 pieces are aligned either horizontally, vertically or diagonally. In this example, the white pieces are diagonally aligned thus the player with the white pieces becomes the Winner.

Another goal here is to prevent your opponent from moving his pieces towards the correct positions so that he cannot form a straight path. Counter move is also important here to prevent your oppenent from making straight while enhancing your positioning on the board.

The Rules and Mechanics

The rules/mechanics are simple.

  • Each player has only one move at a time
  • Each piece you have can only move or travel one square at a time. It can be...
    • One square to the top or bottom or...
    • One square to the left or right or...
    • One square up or down diagonally
  • If the player's move is invalid, a warning will be raise to that player
  • If you reach 2 warnings then you lose the game.
  • The first who aligned the pieces in a straight path will be the Winner

There are 2 Game Modes in this game

  • PvP or Player vs Player and...
  • vs AI or Player vs Artificial Intelligence
In vs AI mode, you are playing against the computer, you can play with different levels from 1 to 4
